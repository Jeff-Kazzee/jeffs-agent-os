# The Little AI Company

## Overview
Jeff's indie software company based in Salmon, Idaho. Building dev tools, design tools, and productivity apps for indie hackers, solo builders, and vibe coders.

## Focus Areas
- Developer tools
- Design tools
- Productivity apps
- AI-assisted development workflows

## Target Audience
- Indie hackers
- Solo builders
- Vibe coders (developers who use AI agents as primary coding partners)

## Brand Values
- Ship fast, iterate based on feedback
- Tools that respect the user's time and energy
- Power-user focused, not beginner-focused
- Local-first when possible
